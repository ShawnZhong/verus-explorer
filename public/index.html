verus-explorer

Real vir + air (from this Verus tree) compiled to wasm32, driving a self-hosted single-threaded Z3 wasm. The button parses an AIR script via air::parser, runs it through air::context::Context::command(), and reports the verdict — SMT goes through Verus's real machinery, not a hardcoded string. No SharedArrayBuffer, no COOP/COEP — works from any static host.
