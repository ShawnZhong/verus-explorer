rustc_interface::run_compiler → HIR (vendored, wasm32)

Rustc front-end invoked directly through rustc_interface::run_compiler (skipping rustc_driver's CLI/ICE/panic-hook glue), source fed via Input::Str, driven past crate-root parsing into create_and_enter_global_ctxt + tcx.resolver_for_lowering() to force macro expansion, name resolution, and HIR lowering. Dumps AST top-level items and HIR free-item def-paths. #![no_core] + #![feature(no_core)] are injected via -Zcrate-attr to suppress the auto extern crate core/std — no sysroot metadata is bundled, so anything touching lang items (+, Sized, generics) won't typeck.
